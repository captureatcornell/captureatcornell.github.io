Festival Happenings...

At our shops!

15 STEPS: gift shop on the Commons now sells specialty foods. On Friday & Saturday, during the festival, the shop will be offering samplings from Brooklyn-based shop Annie's Ginger Elixer, specialty oils and gourmets salts from Durant based out of Oregon, artisanal brined products from Crisp & Co from Eastern PA, samples of Locally Seasoned Tomato Apple Chutney.

Alphabet Soup: Learn to Knit Kit Giveaway & this shop will also be giving away Surprise Web Coupons with every purchase in-store to help people get their holiday shopping started!

At Press Bay/Cayuga St!

  • The Gallery: Will be doing a soft-open in the former One Ring Donuts space just in time for the festival. We expect hours to be Friday, Sept. 30 from 4-8pm, and Saturday & Sunday from 11am-6pm. Exciting flavor combos will be announced soon, stop by and say hello! We are grateful to One Ring and all the flavorful fun they brought to Press Bay over the years, and are happy that the donut tradition will continue onward. Congratulations to everyone on their new journeys!
  • Mama Said Hand Pies:Will feature their Irish apple pie.
  • SewGreen be offering a free sewing or knitting gift with every purchase during Apple Harvest weekend, plus a scavenger hunt for kids, with a prize for every participant.
  • Luck Hare BrewingWill also be opening the taproom early on Fri & Sat at noon. They will be featuring Apple Cider Mules & Apple Cider Whiskey Smash, and sponsoring The Whistlin' Dyl Trio live on Friday Sept. 30th 4-7pm in Press Bay Alley.
  • Rashida Sawyer Bakery: Will be offering Hot Apple Cider, Gingersnap Apple Cheesecake, and Vegan Apple Spice Cake, and a 10% discount on select items.
  • Sparks and Embers:Will be hosting Brigh the Witch doing tarot card readings from 4pm - 6pm on Friday.
  • The Haunted ClosetWill pop up their vintage clothing shop in Press Bay Court Saturday & Sunday, weather-permitting.
  • 5SISTERS DANCE HUB (119 S. Cayuga, Suite 301) presents a Silent Disco on Friday, 9/30, from 8-10 pm (see flyer on events page). “Silent Disco is an opportunity for folks to groove in the community and get acquainted with the new space. Please help spread the word, and join if you're around! There will be apple cider, disco lights (!!), and, most importantly, good vibes”.
  • Tompkins County Democrats:Will host a rally & meet-the-candidate event at 5pm on Oct 2 in Press Bay Alley.

Apple Harvest Gift Basket Contest

We have a gift basket full of donated items from downtown stores. We're going to host a pre-festival contest to find a winner for this basket!

Gift Basket Contest: We will post a photo of the gift basket on the Apple Harvest Facebook Event Page. With the posted image, we will include contest information along with the question "What is Your Favorite Thing about our Apple Harvest Festival?" Anyone who offers a response to the post question will be automatically entered to win the gift basket contest. On Thursday, Oct. 29, 2022, we'll randomly pull the name of a winner from those who commented on the post. This winner will be notified via Facebook that they need to pick up the prize at the Apple Harvest Festival information tent during the event!
